♻️ Why Recycle E-Waste?

Recycling e-waste helps reduce landfill waste, prevents toxic pollution, and conserves valuable materials like gold, silver, and copper.

🔄 How to Recycle E-Waste?

  1. Find a Recycling Center: Locate certified e-waste recyclers.
  2. Check Recyclable Items: Laptops, phones, batteries, printers, and more.
  3. Prepare Your E-Waste: Erase personal data and separate items.
